About Power-Up Designer

The Power-Up Designer is a specialized hardware engineering utility designed to automate the creation of voltage rail timing diagrams. When designing complex PCB systems—especially those involving FPGAs, SoCs, or microcontrollers—engineers must strictly adhere to specific power-on reset (POR) and power-down sequencing requirements to prevent latch-up or hardware damage.
